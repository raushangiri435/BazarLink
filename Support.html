Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a vendor?

To register as a vendor, simply click on the "Vendor Registration" button on the homepage and fill out the required information about your shop. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 24-48 hours.

How can I find shops in my area?

On the homepage, click "Find Local Shops." The website will use your browser's location services to show you all the vendors within a 3km radius.

What is the delivery time?

Baazarlink is committed to same-day delivery. Your order should arrive within a few hours of placement, depending on the vendor's location and business hours.

How do I manage my orders as a vendor?

As a registered vendor, you can log in to your Vendor Dashboard. Here, you can view all new orders, manage your inventory, and update order statuses.
